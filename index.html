About Me
Patrick O'Halloran
Senior, Computer Science Major
I am 22 years old and love Computer Science because of its ability to change our world and the way we live. I have worked two Summers (2013 and 2014) at Northrop Grumman which was a humbling experience as I learned not only what it's like to work in a technical environment but also what it means to do work that helps keep our troops overseas safe from harm's way. I have also worked for Professor Dan Garcia at UC Berkeley (Summer 2015) on a small team that developed an autograder for the graphical programming language Snap!. I enjoy seeing the impact only a few of us can have on thousands of people online in our course. Most recently (Summer 2016) I worked as a mobile engineer for Veeva Systems and will be returning to work there full time beginning in August 2017 as an Android/Back-end Java Engineer. In time my free time I love playing and watching basketball, eating good Mexican food (I'm from San Diego, the best city ever!), and going on adventures around the Bay Area with my girlfriend Yifat.
